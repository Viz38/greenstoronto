Financial planning

Financial planning is a process of identifying, assessing and prioritising your financial goals. It helps you to make decisions about how to achieve those goals.

Financial planning can be a complex process because it involves considering many factors including:

  • Your personal circumstances (e.g., age, health status)
  • Your family’s circumstances (e.g., number of dependents)
  • The current economic climate (e.g., interest rates)

Insurance

Insurance is a form of risk management. It protects you against financial loss in case of an accident or illness, such as medical costs and lost wages due to disability. There are many different types of insurance available, including life insurance, home and auto insurance, health care coverage and property damage coverage.

Insurance can be expensive but it’s important to know whether you need it before purchasing it so that you don’t end up paying for something unnecessarily expensive later on down the road!
